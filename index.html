Contributions reflect each feature's weight in the model's linear predictor; they do not imply causation. Interpret together with the overall clinical picture.
Developed from a single-centre retrospective PDAC cohort (n = 104). Internal validation was performed using bootstrap / out-of-bag (OOB) procedures. This tool is for research use only.

Model definitions. M3 (RNAscope-based model) = clinical + histopathology + RNAscope features; M4 (Integrated model) = full model additionally including IHC features. Top contributor in both: RNAscope epithelial-nuclear positivity.

Method. Cox proportional-hazards with Elastic-Net feature selection. Linear predictor lp = Σ βi xi; survival S(t) = S₀(t)exp(lp); death risk = 1 − S(t). Baseline S₀(t) from the full-development-cohort final model (Breslow estimator).

Discrimination (OOB C-index): M3 = 0.71, M4 = 0.73 (aggregated out-of-bag bootstrap predictions). Absolute probabilities come from the final model fitted to the full development cohort. Cohort n = 104, events = 104 (all deaths, no censoring in OS), maximum follow-up ~1391 days. No external validation. Not for individual clinical decision-making.
