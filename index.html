Pancreatic Cancer — Overall Survival Prediction Calculator

Elastic-Net Cox model · Model M3 (default) / M4 (advanced) · predicts death risk and OS probability at 6 / 12 / 18 months

Research use only — NOT validated for clinical use. This calculator was developed using a single-centre cohort of 104 patients and has undergone bootstrap internal validation but no external validation. Model predictions are based on Elastic-Net–penalized Cox regression coefficients. Reported discrimination metrics, including the AUC, were estimated using aggregated out-of-bag predictions from bootstrap validation, whereas the absolute survival probabilities displayed here are generated by the final model fitted to the full development cohort. These predictions are intended solely for research and illustrative purposes and must not be used as the sole basis for individual clinical decisions.

Predicted individual OS curve

Solid line = predicted overall-survival probability; markers = 6 / 12 / 18-month points.

Model definitions. M3 (RNAscope-based model) = clinical + histopathology + RNAscope features; M4 (Integrated model) = full model additionally including IHC features. Top contributor in both: RNAscope epithelial-nuclear positivity.

Method. Cox proportional-hazards with Elastic-Net feature selection. Linear predictor lp = Σ βi xi; survival S(t) = S₀(t)exp(lp); death risk = 1 − S(t). Baseline S₀(t) from the full-sample final model (Breslow estimator).

Cohort n = 104, events = 104 (all deaths, no censoring in OS), maximum follow-up ~1391 days. Single-centre development cohort; bootstrap internal validation, no external validation. Discrimination metrics from aggregated out-of-bag bootstrap predictions; absolute probabilities from the final model fitted to the full development cohort. Coefficients: final Elastic-Net Cox model.
